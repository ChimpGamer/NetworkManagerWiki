## Punishment duration

The punishment duration can be determined using:\


s - second(s)\
m - minute(s)\
h - hour(s)\
d - day(s)\
w - week(s)\
mo - month(s)\
y - year(s)\
\
Example:\
/gtempban ChimpGamer 3w Hacks\
\
This will ban 'ChimpGamer' from the whole network for 3 weeks.
